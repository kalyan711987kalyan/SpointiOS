10.          MAP, LOCATION SERVICE AND FEATURES

 

Timeline and current location - Current Location of a user is detected using their phone GPS. Timeline which is the route a user travels is updated every 60 seconds, which joins the dots creating a line showing the entire route A to B to C to ÉN.

 

It shows the current location and timeline of the past 24 hours (from current time) of a friend or of a person whose account is an open account (not private account).

 

Features:

 

Check in- Allows you to check in to any place on the Google Maps platform. On clicking the place checked into it redirects the user to a Google Search of the place. You can Check In at places already on Google maps or places not registered on Google maps as well, but with no further Google search result if not registered. Used as a social networking platform to share the places a user has visited.

 

Search Friends around you- With a radius slider from 0-50kms, you can now search for users (friends) you are following, or ALL non-private account users. Helping you connect with friends or new people in a given area.

 

Map (Current Location & Timeline)- Located on a userÕs profile page. Run on Google Maps API. It shows you the current location and timeline (route) travelled by a user for the last 24 hours (T-24). Location services off. When a userÕs location services are off, there will be no timeline updates as well as it will show the last current location before the location services were switched off, hence again not updating the userÕs current location.

 

Groups- On creating a group of users, for e.g. group of all family members or group of your colleagues. You will be able to view all their current locations at once. Helping you know where everyone in the group is. Can be used to plan what time to meet someone (ETA). Also on clicking the direct button option helps you get to either of the users using google maps.
